<span class="title">Everybody is Unique</span>

Each of us is one of a kind. I just don't get it when people say "You should be like this", "You should be like that", etc. And you should follow them because you have to.

I mean, isn't it annoying? Tiring especially when <em>a lot</em> of people is telling you <em>a lot of different</em> things.
